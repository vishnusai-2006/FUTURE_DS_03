# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## Objective
Analyze marketing funnel performance to identify conversion rates, drop-off stages, and channel effectiveness.

---

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Google Colab

---

## Dataset
Olist Marketing Funnel Dataset

Files Used:
- olist_marketing_qualified_leads_dataset.csv
- olist_closed_deals_dataset.csv

---

## Funnel Overview

- Total Leads: 8000
- Closed Deals: 842
- Overall Conversion Rate: ~10.5%

There is a significant drop-off between marketing qualified leads and closed deals.

---

## Channel Performance Insights

Highest Conversion Channels:
- Paid Search (~12.3%)
- Organic Search (~11.8%)
- Direct Traffic (~11.2%)

Low Performing Channels:
- Email (~3.0%)
- Other (~2.6%)
- Display (~5.0%)

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in paid search and SEO.
- Improve email campaign personalization.
- Analyze sales process to reduce funnel drop-off.
- Optimize underperforming channels.

---

## Conclusion

Marketing funnel efficiency can be improved by focusing on high-performing acquisition channels and optimizing the conversion process between lead qualification and deal closure.
